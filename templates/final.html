Blues

Blues has a distinct melancholic and somber tone, which is achieved through vocal techniques, rhythmic techniques and other instrumental techniques. This ensemble usually accompanies a leading vocalist.

Classical

Classical music generally refers to the formal musical tradition of the Western world. Classical music is often characterized by complexity in its musical form and harmonic organization

Country

Country is a popular genre of music. Country music often consists of ballads and dance tunes with generally simple forms, folk lyrics, and harmonies often accompanied by string instruments

Jazz

Jazz music relaxes the body by activating your alpha brain waves. It involves guitar, bass, piano, harmonica, drums, saxophone, vocals.

Metal

Metal, genre of music that includes a group of related styles that are intense, virtuosic, and powerful. Driven by the aggressive sounds of the distorted electric guitar.

Pop

Music usually played on electronic instruments, that is popular with many people because of its strong beat and simple tunes that are easy to remember.
